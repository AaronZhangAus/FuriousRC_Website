OUR CARS

MJX 10303/10304 1/10 Rally Car

The MJX 10303 and 10304 are exciting 1/10 scale 4WD rally cars that bring real rally-style driving to the Furious RC track.

With authentic WRC-inspired bodies, powerful brushless performance, and stable all-wheel-drive handling, they are perfect for racers who love speed, sliding, cornering, and close racing action.

  • Realistic rally look
  • 1/10 scale size
  • 4WD drivetrain
  • Brushless 3652kv sensored motor
  • Gyro-assisted stability
  • Strong aluminium chassis
  • Oil-filled suspension
  • Full LED lighting system
  • Hobby-grade racing feel

MJX 14209 Desert Truck

The MJX 14209 Hyper Go is a compact, high-performance 1/14 scale brushless 4WD off-road truck designed for fast, exciting RC racing.

With strong traction, responsive handling, oil-filled suspension, LED lights, and a ready-to-run setup, it is a great choice for energetic race sessions, off-road driving demonstrations, and beginner-friendly motorsport fun.

  • Brushless 4WD Performance
  • Run fast while easy to handle
  • Strong Off-Road Handling
  • Oil-Filled Suspension
  • Durable Drivetrain
  • LED Light System
  • High-Grip Off-Road Tyres

Arrma Fury 223S Short Course Truck

The ARRMA Fury 223S BLX is a tough, fast 1/10 scale 2WD short course truck built for exciting RC racing and off-road action.

With brushless power, 2S/3S battery capability, stability assistance, oil-filled suspension, and a durable truck body, it gives young racers a thrilling short course racing experience while remaining manageable under supervision.

  • Motor: Spektrum Firma 3650 4000KV Brushless
  • Drive Type: 2WD Short Course Truck
  • Stability Assist: Spektrum DSC Gyro System
  • Suspension: Oil-Filled Coil-Over Shocks
  • Chassis: Durable Composite Construction
  • Electronics: Waterproof Spektrum System
