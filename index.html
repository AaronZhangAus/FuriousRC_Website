Perfect for
School Incursions

Furious RC thoughtfully designs a unique, educational program "Bigfoot RC Soccer Arena", an action-packed school STEM incursion that introduces students to the exciting world of Radio Control Car through hands-on learning and action-packed 2v2 soccer games.

The program begins with an engaging crash course showcasing the key components of a modern electric RC car, radio signal transmitting/receiving, throttle and steering control, driving etiquette and safety rules. Students then step into a fenced soccer court for a fast-moving 2 vs 2 RC soccer game using highly detailed Bigfoot monster trucks.

It is a fun and memorable way for students to develop hand-eye coordination, problem-solving skills, confidence and teamwork while discovering how mechanical and electrical ideas work together in real RC vehicles.

Furious RC Mega Race package brings the excitement of mini motorsport directly to your venue, creating a unique birthday experience where children can drive, practise, race, and cheer each other on in a safe and exciting environment.
