1 Hour Furious RC Beginner Racer Training Camp

A fast-paced, action-packed 60-minute hands-on RC racing experience designed for young beginners.

Racers will learn the basics of RC car racing, practise essential driving skills, and take part in exhilarating race challenges using a variety of race-tuned RC vehicles, including rally cars, short course trucks, Bigfoot trucks.

The session is supervised by a Chief Instructor and a Training Assistant to ensure a fun, safe, and well-managed experience.

Package Details

Price: $450
Group Size: 6–10 racers
Recommended Age: 6–12 years old
Duration: 60 minutes
Staffing: 1 Chief Instructor + 1 Training Assistant

Training Schedule

  • Types of Common RC Cars
  • RC Cars 101: Anatomy of An Electric RC Car
  • Sounds & Tyres
  • Vehicle Showcase: Rally Car, Short Course Truck, Bigfoot Truck, Rock Crawler
  • Racing Etiquette and Safety Rules
  • Guided Practice Run
  • Race Challenge
  • Winner Announcement and Awards

What We Provide

We bring everything required for the camp, including:

  • 5–8 different types of ready-to-race RC cars
  • 2.4GHz RC controllers
  • 30 x 5000mAh 2S batteries and balance chargers
  • RC car parts and modules for demonstration
  • Track barriers and racing equipment
  • First aid kit

2 Hour Furious RC Mega Race

A high-energy 120-minute RC racing activation designed for community events, family carnivals, play days, school fun days, and public events.

The Furious RC Mega Race delivers a continuous flow of racing excitement where event-goers line up, wait for their turn, and join the next available race session. Each session gives participants a quick introduction, practice time, and an exhilarating race experience.

Each race session runs for 6 minutes and includes 5 racers at a time. Participants receive a brief controller introduction and practice run before competing in a friendly 3-minute race challenge. This format keeps the activity organised, easy to manage, and exciting for both racers and spectators.

Package Details

Duration: 120 minutes
Format: Queue-based race sessions
Racers per Session: 5 participants
Session Length: 6 minutes
Recommended Age: 6+ years old
Suitable For: Council events, festivals, family carnivals, school fun days and community activations
Staffing: 1 Chief Instructor + 1 Training Assistant
Pricing: Contact us for event pricing

Event Format

Each race session includes:

  • 1 minute: quick controller introduction, racers get ready
  • 2 minutes: a guided practice run
  • 3 minutes: race challenge

5 racers per session
Participants line up and wait for the next available race
Staff manage the queue, safety rules, race starts, and session changeovers
Racing continues throughout the 120-minute activation
Battery swaps every two sessions, track checks are handled by Furious RC staff in the background
